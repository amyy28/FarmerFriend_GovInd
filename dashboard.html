OUR OBJECTIVE: To provide solutions keeping in mind inclusivity (agriculture is becoming feminine and has huge percentage of small farm holding) and to focus on the farmer’s Balance sheet while encouraging youth/start ups by sustaining their operations & scaling up.
Methodology

Develop a National Digital Backbone including a National Repository of Scientific Research, Technologies, ICT and AI based user friendly platforms keeping Industry, Incubators, Venture Capitalists and Government as enablers and connecting to the farmers as end users.
